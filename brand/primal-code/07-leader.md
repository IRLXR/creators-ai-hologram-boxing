# Pillar 7 — Leader

**Primal Code:** A central figure or ethos that embodies the vision and speaks for the community.

**Built on:** **Our turn.**

Every generation needs a voice that says: **"This is ours now."** Our Leader isn't one celebrity — it's the **ethos of a generation showing up together**: Creators AI, the host, Wave, Founding Fans, and every belonger who passes it on.
