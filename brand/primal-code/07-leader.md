# Pillar 7 — Leader

**Primal Code:** A central figure or ethos that embodies the vision and speaks for the community.

---

## What it is (plain English)

Belief systems need a face — or a voice. The Leader is who fans **trust to speak for the tribe**: the creator, host, founder energy, or brand persona that says *"Follow me inside the tent."*

For Creators AI Hologram Boxing, leadership is **distributed**: the Creators AI ethos, the UGC host, the Wave roster, and the community of Founding Fans all play a role.

---

## Primary leader: Creators AI (the brand ethos)

**Creators AI** is the league name and the creative force behind hologram boxing.

| Attribute | Expression |
|-----------|------------|
| **Vision** | Invent live entertainment people walk into — not just watch |
| **Voice** | Premium, cinematic, confident, welcoming |
| **Platform presence** | Kick + Twitch `@creatorsai` — streams when fights drop |
| **Contact** | `ic4d@irlxr.com` |
| **Site** | creators-ai-hologram-boxing.vercel.app |

The brand speaks in **trailer voice** in ads and **conversational UGC** in social scripts — same vision, two registers.

---

## Secondary leader: The UGC host (human face)

The recurring **social / ad host** (lavender crop top, gold jewelry, white-plate UGC style) humanizes the league for TikTok and Instagram.

**Role:**
- Explain hologram boxing in plain language
- Translate sacred words for new audiences
- Drive CTAs — *tap to book*, *link in bio*, *Become a Founding Fan*

**Where defined:** `ugc/01-what-is-hologram-boxing.md`, `assets/CHARACTER.md`, Brief 003 avatar keyframes.

**Leader behavior:** Direct eye contact, mic to mouth, confident but approachable — *your friend who already walked inside and came back to tell you.*

---

## Tertiary leader: Wave (character mythology)

**Wave** is not a single person — it is the **fighter line** that carries competitive drama.

- Wave Gold · Wave Fire · Wave Blue · Wave Silver · Wave Green · Wave Pink
- Faceless wireframe — fans project onto **colors and matchups**
- **Pick your fighter** ritual makes Wave a participatory leader, not a celebrity

**Copy hook:** *"Meet Wave. Only the beginning."*

Wave leads through **matchups and countdowns**, not interviews or personal scandal.

## Leader voice guidelines

### Ad / trailer register (Orbitron energy)

> *"Picture this — you walk into the arena, gear up, throw on the headset… and BOOM — you're front row."*

Use for: 15s/60s ads, landing hero, end cards, Social Collection headlines.

### UGC / social register (Gen Z conversational)

> *"Real talk — it's not what you think. You don't fight. You watch AI boxers in a tent."*

Use for: TikTok scripts, Reels host plates, FAQ explainers.

### Official / press register

> *"Creators AI Hologram Boxing is the world's first live AI hologram boxing experience — an inflatable tent, AR headsets, and Wave fighters in human-scale combat on a glowing floor square."*

Use for: partners, press, `about.html`, manager briefs.

---

## What the leader never does

- Claim to recreate real celebrity fights or real PPV history
- Speak down to the audience or over-explain AI jargon
- Promise what the tent experience cannot deliver
- Disappear after launch — leader presence continues via streams, episodes, and fight cards
