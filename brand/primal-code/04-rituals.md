# Pillar 4 — Rituals

**Primal Code:** Repeatable behaviors that actively engage people with the brand.

**Built on:** **Our turn.**

---

## What it is (plain English)

Rituals are how a generation **proves it belongs**. Like school chants, game-day traditions, or national holidays — the same motions repeated until they mean *us*.

Our rituals say: **we're not watching someone else's chapter. We're living ours.**

---

## Core rituals

### Ritual 1 — Claim your turn (Founding Fan)

**Before the world catches on.**

1. Enter email on landing page
2. **Submitting → Submitted → thank-you video**
3. Say without shame: *"I was here early. Our turn."*

**Copy hook:** *Become a Founding Fan — join before launch.*

---

### Ritual 2 — Walk inside

**Cross into our chapter.**

1. Approach the black tent at night
2. Pass the **blue-lit entrance**
3. Step into fog and the glowing square

**Copy hook:** *Would you walk inside?*

---

### Ritual 3 — Gear up

**Same generation, same headset, same night.**

1. Strap on the white AR headset
2. Face the square with the crowd
3. Feel it before you explain it

**Copy hook:** *One headset. Our turn.*

---

### Ritual 4 — Pick your Wave

**Choose a side. Care about the outcome.**

1. Browse Wave roster · enter influencer code
2. Wait for the **countdown**
3. Tickets unlock when the clock hits zero

**Copy hook:** *Pick your fighter — our fight, our stakes.*

---

### Ritual 5 — Countdown together

**Shared calendar — like game day.**

1. Watch **Next Event** timer (landing, site, fight cards)
2. Stream on Kick/Twitch while you wait
3. Show up when tickets go live

**Copy hook:** *HB 002 — our turn starts when the countdown hits zero.*

---

### Ritual 6 — Stream, then pull up

**Remote belongers become tent belongers.**

1. Watch free on Kick/Twitch
2. Feel the crowd through the feed
3. Pull up IRL when you're ready for the full night

**Copy hook:** *Stream it. Then show up. Our turn doesn't wait forever.*

---

### Ritual 7 — Pass it on

**Generational handoff inside the tribe.**

1. Walk out of the tent changed
2. Post the clip · tell your crew · share the code
3. Bring someone who hasn't had their turn yet

**Copy hook:** *One night can change you — pass it on.*

## Ritual design principles

1. **Generational** — same motion, new fight, new year of belongers
2. **Participatory** — you don't watch our turn; you're in it
3. **Timed** — countdowns create *our* shared calendar
4. **Shareable** — every ritual produces a story someone else wants in on

---

## What to avoid

- Rituals that feel like **last era's** PPV hype (aggressive gatekeeping)
- Skipping the Evolution → tent handoff in generational campaigns
- One-off stunts with no sequel
