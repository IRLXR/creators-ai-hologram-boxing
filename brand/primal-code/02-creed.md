# Pillar 2 — Creed

**Primal Code:** The guiding mission, values, or motto that people believe in and repeat.

---

## What it is (plain English)

The Creed is what Founding Fans **stand for**. It is not a mission statement buried on a corporate page. It is the belief people join when they tap **Notify Me**, share a Reel, or tell a friend *"you have to walk inside."*

---

## Our creed (official)

### Mission

**Make live entertainment feel personal again — through AI hologram boxing you experience in the tent, not on a screen.**

### Values

| Value | What it means |
|-------|----------------|
| **Experience over spectacle** | We sell the feeling of being front row inside the tent — not hype for hype's sake. |
| **Premium, not gimmick** | Cinematic lighting, real boxing motion, disciplined visuals — never cheap novelty. |
| **Inclusive fight night** | Family-friendly energy. All ages can walk in. No gatekeeping the future. |
| **Creator-first culture** | Built for people who stream, share, and show up — Kick, Twitch, social, IRL. |
| **Original mythology** | Wave fighters, original matchups, our tent — never counterfeit celebrity fights. |

### Motto

**Step in. Gear up. Watch hologram boxing.**

### Rally cry (CTA)

**Become a Founding Fan.**

## Beliefs our community repeats

These are the sentences fans should be able to finish:

1. *"The future of live entertainment isn't coming — it's **here**."*
2. *"You don't fight. You **watch** — but you're **inside** the experience."*
3. *"One headset. **Infinite possibilities**."*
4. *"You had to **be there**."*
5. *"Every generation changes the game. **Now it's our turn**."*

## Creed in one paragraph (for bios and decks)

Creators AI Hologram Boxing believes the next era of live entertainment is **experiential, immersive, and shared in real life** — not trapped on a phone. We bring people into a premium inflatable tent, put AR headsets on every guest, and let them witness **Wave** hologram fighters in human-scale combat on a glowing floor square. We are building a league, a culture, and a community of Founding Fans who saw it first.

## Tone rules (from Brand Bible)

- Premium · Modern · Cinematic · Exclusive
- Confident, not arrogant
- Wonder first, tech second

---

## What to avoid

- Empty superlatives ("best ever," "revolutionary") without showing the tent
- Crypto-first messaging ahead of experience (MVM supports tickets; experience leads)
- Aggressive "us vs them" without pointing to **Nonbelievers** pillar intentionally
