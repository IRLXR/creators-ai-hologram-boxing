# Pillar 2 — Creed

**Primal Code:** The guiding mission, values, or motto that people believe in and repeat.

**Built on:** **Our turn.**

---

## What it is (plain English)

The Creed is what **every belonger** stands for — Founding Fans, streamers, headset guests, ringside attendees, families, creators, partners. Not a corporate mission buried on a page. A **generational promise** people repeat like school pride or a country motto.

---

## Master motto

> **Our turn.**

---

## Extended creed (two sentences — use in bios, decks, VO)

**This is our generation's turn.** Not better than the past. Not copying it. The next chapter of fight night — and we're living it while it's still new.

---

## Supporting motto (action line)

**Step in. Gear up. Watch hologram boxing.**

---

## Rally cry (CTA)

**Become a Founding Fan.**

---

## Five shared tribal values

Each value is a **different door** into the same house. All flow from **Our turn.**

## Tone rules

- Generational pride, not arrogance
- Premium · cinematic · human
- Honor history → claim the future
- Wonder first, tech second

---

## What to avoid

- "We destroyed the old way" — we **continue the evolution**
- Empty hype without showing the tent
- Making Founding Fans feel superior — they were just **early**, not exclusive forever

---

- End cards, Social Collection 006, Brief 003 CTA
- Merch, bio links, countdown posts
- Internal review: *Does this sound like **Our turn** — or generic AI marketing?*
