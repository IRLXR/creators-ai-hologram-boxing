## Our creation story

For centuries, fight night evolved — campfires, colosseums, arenas, pay-per-view. Each generation changed the game. Then the game stopped changing.

Screens got sharper. Crowds got smaller. The feeling of *being there* faded into clips and couch streams. A whole generation grew up consuming entertainment alone — scrolling past moments instead of living them.

Creators AI Hologram Boxing started when we asked a different question:

> **What if this generation didn't wait for someone else to invent the next fight night — what if we built it ourselves?**

The answer was a **portable inflatable tent** at night — fog, quilted walls, a glowing square on the floor. Real people. AR headsets on every guest. **Wave** hologram fighters spar human-sized inside the square. Not a copy of boxing history. Not a VR app on a couch. **A new chapter — written by the people showing up while it's still new.**

Founding Fans didn't join a waitlist. They raised their hand and said: **Our turn.**
