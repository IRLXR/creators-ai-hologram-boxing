# Pillar 5 — Sacred Words

**Primal Code:** Unique vocabulary and internal language that the community understands and uses — reinforcing who belongs.

---

## What it is (plain English)

Sacred Words are **insider terms**. When a Founding Fan says *"I'm pulling up to the tent for HB 002"* — everyone in the tribe knows exactly what that means. This language separates fans from tourists.

---

## Core sacred words (always capitalize in brand copy)

| Term | Meaning | Example usage |
|------|---------|----------------|
| **Hologram Boxing** | The category we invented — live AI hologram fights in a tent | "Have you seen hologram boxing yet?" |
| **The Tent** | The premium inflatable arena — interior + exterior | "Meet me at the tent." |
| **Walk Inside** | The threshold ritual — entering the experience | "Would you walk inside?" |
| **Gear Up** | Put on the AR headset | "Gear up — fight's about to start." |
| **Wave** | Our hologram fighter line — wireframe, WAVE waistband | "Meet Wave." / "Wave Gold vs Wave Fire" |
| **The Square** | Glowing blue-purple floor grid where fighters spar | "They're inside the square." |
| **Founding Fan** | Early believer — waitlist member before mass launch | "Become a Founding Fan." |
| **HB 002** | Event / fight card shorthand (Hologram Boxing 002) | "HB 002 countdown is live." |
| **Pull Up** | Come IRL to the tent after streaming online | "Stream it free — pull up when you're ready." |
| **Front Row** | Headset POV — immersive ringside energy | "You're front row without being in the ring." |
| **You Had To Be There** | Post-event FOMO + social proof | "You had to be there." |

## Fighter sacred names (Wave roster)

Never generic "boxer" when referring to our roster — use **Wave [Color]**:

- Wave Gold · Wave Fire · Wave Blue · Wave Silver · Wave Green · Wave Pink

## Phrases fans should echo (approved slang)

✅ Use:
- "It's giving fight night plus future tech."
- "No cap — you're inside the tent."
- "The future isn't coming. It's here."
- "Every generation changes the game."
- "Only the beginning." (Wave intro)
- "Link in bio — Founding Fan."

❌ Avoid in brand voice:
- "Metaverse boxing" (we are IRL tent-first)
- "Virtual reality game" (not a game — live entertainment)
- Celebrity fight names or real PPV shorthand
- "Cheap tickets" / discount language (premium positioning)

## How sacred words spread

1. **Repeat in every ad VO and end card**
2. **Caption templates** in `ugc/AD-COPY.md` and Social Collection brief
3. **On-screen text** in Reels — `THE TENT`, `GEAR UP`, `WAVE`, `FOUNDING FAN`
4. **Staff / host language** in UGC scripts — teach viewers the vocabulary by using it naturally
