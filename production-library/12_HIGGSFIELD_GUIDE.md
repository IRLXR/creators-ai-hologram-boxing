# 12 — HIGGSFIELD GUIDE

## Model

**`seedance_2_0` only** for all video. Never Kling.

## Responsibilities

- Animate approved Nano Banana frames only.
- Use approved keyframes as `start_image` whenever supported.
- Preserve composition, camera angle, lighting, lens, character positioning, headset, arena, Wave, color, mood, scale.
- Smooth cinematic motion only — no morphing, flickering, or redesign during animation.
- Generate one video shot at a time; verify before next shot.
- Regenerate only for major issues (broken anatomy, wrong branding, severe artifacts, unusable camera).

## Credit optimization

- Do not animate until Nano Banana frames are approved.
- Reuse approved prompts and environments.
- Never create alternate versions unless requested.
