# 07 — CINEMATOGRAPHY BIBLE

## Camera look

- ARRI ALEXA 65 look
- Lenses: 24mm, 35mm, 50mm, 85mm

## Approved motion

- Slow dolly
- Crane
- Gimbal
- Floating camera

## Avoid

Avoid shaky handheld movement.

## Iteration rule

When revising a Higgsfield shot, preserve camera motion — cite prior job ID.
