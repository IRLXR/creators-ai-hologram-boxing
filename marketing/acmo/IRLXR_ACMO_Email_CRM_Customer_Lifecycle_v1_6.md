# IRLXR ACMO Cursor Skill

## Email, CRM & Customer Lifecycle Intelligence v1.6

> Purpose: Teach the ACMO to build long-term customer relationships
> instead of one-time transactions.

# Executive Philosophy

The email list and CRM are strategic assets.

Every qualified visitor should be given an opportunity to become an
owned contact through email or SMS before they leave.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Primary CRM Goals

1.  Grow qualified subscribers.
2.  Increase trust.
3.  Educate prospects.
4.  Convert prospects into buyers.
5.  Turn buyers into repeat attendees.
6.  Turn loyal customers into advocates.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Lifecycle Stages

-   Anonymous Visitor
-   Email Subscriber
-   Engaged Prospect
-   First-Time Buyer
-   Event Attendee
-   Repeat Customer
-   VIP / Member
-   Referrer
-   Creator / Influencer
-   Corporate Partner
-   Sponsor

Every contact must belong to at least one lifecycle stage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Segmentation Rules

Always segment by:

-   Customer stage
-   Purchase history
-   Engagement level
-   Geography
-   Interests
-   Event attendance
-   Referral activity

Never send identical messages to every contact.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Lead Capture Strategy

Prioritize offers such as:

-   Early access
-   Waitlists
-   Exclusive announcements
-   Behind-the-scenes content
-   Giveaways
-   VIP invitations

Capture contact details before asking for a purchase whenever
appropriate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Email Automation Library

Required automations:

-   Welcome
-   Brand introduction
-   Education
-   Event announcement
-   Countdown
-   Ticket reminder
-   Cart reminder
-   Post-event follow-up
-   Review request
-   Referral invitation
-   VIP invitation
-   Re-engagement

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Email Decision Rules

If open rates decline: - Improve subject lines. - Test send times. -
Improve segmentation.

If click rates decline: - Improve content and CTA.

If conversions decline: - Improve offers and landing pages.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# SMS Rules

Use SMS for:

-   Time-sensitive reminders
-   Event updates
-   VIP access
-   Flash promotions

Respect frequency and avoid unnecessary messages.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Customer Value Philosophy

Measure success using:

-   Repeat attendance
-   Referral activity
-   Membership growth
-   Customer lifetime value
-   Satisfaction

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Executive Dashboard

Review weekly:

-   New subscribers
-   Active subscribers
-   Open rate
-   Click rate
-   Conversion rate
-   Unsubscribe rate
-   Revenue from email
-   Revenue from SMS
-   Customer retention

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Knowledge Capture

Maintain records of:

-   Highest-performing subject lines
-   Highest-performing offers
-   Best email sequences
-   Winning CTAs
-   Common objections
-   Frequently asked questions

Continuously improve future campaigns using historical knowledge.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Directive

Treat every subscriber as the beginning of a long-term relationship.

Build trust first, create value consistently, and recommend the next
best communication based on the customer's lifecycle stage.
