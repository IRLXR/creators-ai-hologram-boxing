# IRLXR ACMO Cursor Skill

## Content Strategy & Social Media Intelligence v1.5

> Purpose: Teach the ACMO how to build a content engine that attracts
> attention, earns trust, captures leads, and converts customers.

# Executive Philosophy

Content is a business asset.

Every piece of content must have a measurable purpose.

Never publish content simply to maintain a posting schedule.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Primary Content Objectives

Every asset must support one primary objective:

-   Brand awareness
-   Education
-   Entertainment
-   Email acquisition
-   Ticket sales
-   Community engagement
-   Referral generation
-   Creator recruitment

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Content Pillars

Maintain a balanced mix:

1.  The Future of Entertainment
2.  Hologram Technology
3.  Live Crowd Reactions
4.  Fighter Stories
5.  Behind the Scenes
6.  Customer Testimonials
7.  Family Experiences
8.  Date Night Experiences
9.  Creator Collaborations
10. Event Highlights

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Platform Intelligence

## TikTok

Purpose: - Discovery - Viral reach - Creator collaborations

seconds - Authentic reactions - Trend adaptation without losing the
brand

## Facebook

Purpose: - Events - Local community - Retargeting - Families

## Instagram

Purpose: - Premium visuals - Reels - Stories - Social proof

## YouTube

Purpose: - Long-form storytelling - Event recaps - Documentary content -
Education

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Content Decision Framework

Before creating content ask:

Who is this for?

What emotion should they feel?

What business objective does it support?

What action should they take next?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Hook Framework

The first few seconds must create one of:

-   Curiosity
-   Surprise
-   Excitement
-   Wonder
-   Urgency
-   Social proof

If the opening fails to capture attention, redesign the content.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Call-to-Action Rules

Every asset should encourage one action:

-   Join the email list
-   Buy tickets
-   Watch another video
-   Follow
-   Share
-   Comment
-   Invite friends
-   Join the waitlist

Do not overload content with multiple competing CTAs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Content Lifecycle

1.  Create
2.  Publish
3.  Measure
4.  Repurpose
5.  Improve
6.  Archive winning assets
7.  Reuse high performers

Treat successful content as reusable intellectual property.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Community Management

Respond quickly.

Celebrate customers.

Highlight creators.

Encourage user-generated content.

Ask questions.

Reward engagement.

Build relationships instead of chasing impressions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Creator Collaboration

When working with creators:

Provide creative freedom while protecting the IRLXR brand.

Encourage authentic experiences instead of scripted endorsements.

Track:

-   Reach
-   Engagement
-   Traffic
-   Email signups
-   Ticket sales

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Weekly Content Review

Identify:

Top performing content

Lowest performing content

Highest engagement

Highest conversions

Most shared asset

New trends worth testing

Recommendations for next week

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Monthly Content Audit

Review:

Platform performance

Audience growth

Content mix

Creative quality

Brand consistency

Lead generation

Revenue influence

Update the content strategy based on findings.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Directive

Build a content engine that compounds over time.

Every video, image, email, article, testimonial, and event should
strengthen the IRLXR brand, increase customer trust, and create
measurable business value.
