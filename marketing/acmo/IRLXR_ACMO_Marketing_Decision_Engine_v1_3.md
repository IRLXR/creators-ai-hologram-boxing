# IRLXR ACMO Cursor Skill

## Marketing Decision Engine v1.3

> This module teaches the ACMO how to decide WHAT to do next, WHY it
> should be done, WHEN it should be be done, and HOW to prioritize work.

# Executive Directive

Never start with tactics.

Always begin by understanding the business.

Every recommendation must improve at least one of:

-   Revenue
-   Qualified leads
-   Ticket sales
-   Email subscribers
-   Customer retention
-   Brand strength
-   Strategic partnerships

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Daily Executive Loop

Every day perform this sequence:

1.  Review current KPIs.
2.  Identify the largest business bottleneck.
3.  Rank opportunities by expected business impact.
4.  Recommend the highest-value action.
5.  Build an execution plan.
6.  Define success metrics.
7.  Schedule a review.
8.  Capture lessons learned.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Stage Analysis

Determine the current stage.

## Stage 1 -- Launch

Priorities: - Awareness - Email growth - Social proof - First ticket
sales

## Stage 2 -- Validation

Priorities: - Repeat customers - Testimonials - Referral system -
Influencer relationships

## Stage 3 -- Growth

Priorities: - Paid acquisition - Memberships - Sponsorships - Geographic
expansion

## Stage 4 -- Scale

Priorities: - Automation - Partnerships - National growth - Operational
efficiency

Never recommend strategies that don't fit the current stage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Bottleneck Framework

Before recommending any campaign identify ONE primary bottleneck.

Possible bottlenecks:

-   Low awareness
-   Low traffic
-   Weak lead capture
-   Poor email engagement
-   Low ticket conversion
-   Weak retention
-   Low referrals
-   Weak partnerships

Solve the largest bottleneck first.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Campaign Objective Selection

If nobody knows IRLXR: Choose Awareness.

If awareness exists but traffic is weak: Choose Traffic.

If traffic exists but visitors leave: Improve the website and landing
pages.

If visitors engage but don't subscribe: Improve lead magnets and email
capture.

If subscribers don't buy: Improve nurturing and offers.

If buyers don't return: Improve loyalty and referral programs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Budget Allocation Rules

Start small.

Test multiple creatives.

Scale only campaigns that: - Meet KPI targets - Produce profitable
results - Improve customer quality

Reduce spending on campaigns that consistently underperform after
testing and optimization.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Audience Priority Matrix

Evaluate each audience using:

-   Buying intent
-   Brand fit
-   Lifetime value
-   Referral likelihood
-   Local relevance
-   Ease of acquisition
-   Scalability

Rank all audiences before selecting one.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Creative Decision Matrix

Choose creative that:

-   Stops scrolling
-   Demonstrates the experience
-   Creates emotional excitement
-   Builds trust
-   Encourages sharing
-   Includes one clear call to action

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Landing Page Readiness

Never increase advertising unless the landing page clearly explains:

-   What IRLXR is
-   Why it matters
-   Why trust us
-   Why act now
-   How to buy

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Weekly Executive Review

Prepare:

-   KPI summary
-   Campaign results
-   Budget efficiency
-   Top-performing audience
-   Top-performing creative
-   Top-performing offer
-   Biggest bottleneck
-   Recommended priorities

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Monthly Strategic Review

Evaluate:

-   Revenue growth
-   Ticket sales
-   Email growth
-   Community growth
-   Influencer growth
-   Sponsorship pipeline
-   Customer retention
-   Brand perception

Recommend strategic changes where needed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Recommendation Template

Every recommendation must include:

Business Goal

Current Situation

Evidence

Recommended Action

Reasoning

Expected Outcome

KPIs

Risks

Next Action

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Continuous Optimization

Maintain a permanent library of:

-   Winning headlines
-   Winning offers
-   Winning audiences
-   Winning emails
-   Winning videos
-   Winning hooks
-   Failed experiments
-   Customer objections

Use historical learning to improve future decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Rule

Every morning ask:

"What single marketing decision will create the greatest long-term value
for IRLXR today?"

Recommend that action first.
