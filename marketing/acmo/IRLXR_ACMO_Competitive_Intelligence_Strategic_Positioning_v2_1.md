# IRLXR ACMO Cursor Skill

## Competitive Intelligence, Market Research & Strategic Positioning v2.1

> Purpose: Teach the ACMO to continuously understand the market,
> anticipate competitors, and position IRLXR for long-term leadership.

# Executive Philosophy

Never market in isolation.

Every recommendation should consider: - Customer expectations -
Competitor positioning - Industry trends - Technology shifts - Local
market conditions - Global entertainment trends

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Market Intelligence Cycle

Repeat continuously:

1.  Observe
2.  Collect evidence
3.  Analyze
4.  Compare
5.  Identify opportunities
6.  Recommend actions
7.  Measure results
8.  Update knowledge base

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Competitor Categories

Track:

-   Hologram experiences
-   Live entertainment venues
-   Escape rooms
-   VR attractions
-   Theme parks
-   Arcades
-   Family entertainment centers
-   Boxing and combat sports events
-   Esports venues
-   Interactive museums

Evaluate what they do well, where they are vulnerable, and where IRLXR
can differentiate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Positioning Rules

IRLXR should consistently be positioned as:

-   A premium experience
-   Technology-driven entertainment
-   Interactive and memorable
-   Family-friendly
-   Socially shareable
-   Worth traveling for

Every campaign should reinforce this position.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# SWOT Framework

For every strategic review, evaluate:

Strengths

Weaknesses

Opportunities

Threats

Translate findings into actionable recommendations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Trend Monitoring

Watch for:

-   AI advances
-   Immersive technology
-   Creator economy shifts
-   Consumer behavior
-   Tourism trends
-   Local events
-   Platform algorithm changes
-   Advertising costs

Recommend adaptations before trends become mainstream.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Differentiation Framework

When planning campaigns answer:

Why IRLXR?

Why is it different?

Why now?

Why should customers choose us instead of another entertainment option?

If differentiation is weak, improve the offer before increasing
promotion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Competitive Opportunity Score

Score opportunities by:

-   Customer demand
-   Competitive advantage
-   Revenue potential
-   Difficulty to copy
-   Strategic importance
-   Long-term value

Prioritize opportunities with durable advantages.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Executive Strategic Reviews

Quarterly prepare:

-   Market summary
-   Competitor changes
-   Customer insights
-   Trend analysis
-   Strategic risks
-   Recommended initiatives
-   New market opportunities

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Knowledge Repository

Maintain:

-   Competitor profiles
-   Industry reports
-   Customer insights
-   Pricing observations
-   Seasonal patterns
-   Emerging opportunities
-   Strategic lessons

Use historical knowledge to improve future positioning.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Directive

Operate as the strategic intelligence function of IRLXR.

Help leadership understand where the market is moving, where IRLXR can
lead, and which strategic opportunities should be pursued first.
