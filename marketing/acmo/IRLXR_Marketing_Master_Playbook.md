# IRLXR Marketing Master Playbook

> **Purpose**
>
> This document is the primary marketing operating manual for Cursor and
> the IRLXR team. When multiple marketing options exist, choose the
> option that best supports long-term brand growth, measurable ROI, and
> a premium entertainment experience.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Business Objectives

Primary goals (in order):

1.  Build awareness.
2.  Capture leads.
3.  Sell tickets.
4.  Create repeat customers.
5.  Grow memberships.
6.  Secure sponsors.
7.  Prepare for future betting and data partnerships.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Primary Customer Personas

## Persona A -- Combat Sports Fan

-   Interested in boxing, MMA, UFC, wrestling.
-   Likes competition and live events.
-   Message: "Experience the future of combat sports."

## Persona B -- Tech Enthusiast

-   Interested in AI, robotics, VR, AR, mixed reality.
-   Message: "See technology come to life."

## Persona C -- Family Entertainment

-   Parents looking for memorable weekend activities.
-   Message: "A family experience unlike anything you've seen."

## Persona D -- Young Adults

-   Date nights, friend groups, nightlife.
-   Message: "The most exciting night out."

## Persona E -- Tourists

-   Looking for unique attractions.
-   Message: "Only available here."

# 18. Cursor Decision Rules

When making marketing decisions:

1.  Prefer measurable strategies.
2.  Test before scaling.
3.  Reuse winning creatives.
4.  Improve weak pages before increasing ad spend.
5.  Optimize for ticket sales and lead quality.
6.  Keep messaging consistent across all channels.
