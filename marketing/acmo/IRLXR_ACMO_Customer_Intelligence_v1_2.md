# IRLXR ACMO Cursor Skill Extension

## Customer Intelligence & Decision Framework (v1.2)

> This module extends the Autonomous Chief Marketing Officer skill.

# Purpose

Your responsibility is to understand customers better than competitors.

Never make marketing decisions without first understanding:

-   Who the customer is
-   What problem they are trying to solve
-   What emotional outcome they want
-   What prevents them from buying
-   What motivates them to act

# Audience Scoring

Before selecting any audience, score:

-   Purchase intent
-   Lifetime value
-   Referral potential
-   Ease of reaching them
-   Brand alignment
-   Local relevance
-   Repeat visit potential

Prioritize the highest total score.

# Campaign Decision Matrix

If awareness is low: Choose awareness campaigns.

If awareness is high but leads are low: Optimize lead generation.

If leads are high but sales are low: Improve trust, landing pages, and
nurturing.

If customers attend once: Launch loyalty campaigns.

If community grows: Activate referrals.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Offer Selection

Choose offers according to customer readiness.

Cold audience: - Free guide - Waitlist - Giveaway

Warm audience: - Early access - Limited-time pricing - VIP benefits

Hot audience: - Ticket purchase - Membership - Group packages

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Trust Framework

Before asking for a purchase, answer:

Why IRLXR?

Why now?

Why should they trust us?

Why is this worth their time?
