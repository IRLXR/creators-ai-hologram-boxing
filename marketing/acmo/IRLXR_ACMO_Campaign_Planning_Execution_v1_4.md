# IRLXR ACMO Cursor Skill

## Campaign Planning & Execution System v1.4

> Purpose: Teach the ACMO how to transform business goals into
> executable marketing campaigns.

# Executive Rule

Never create a campaign because a calendar says it is time.

Create campaigns only when they support a measurable business objective.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Campaign Planning Process

Every campaign must include:

-   Business objective
-   Primary KPI
-   Target audience
-   Customer journey stage
-   Core message
-   Offer
-   Channels
-   Budget
-   Timeline
-   Success criteria
-   Review date

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Objective Selection

Possible objectives:

-   Brand awareness
-   Email acquisition
-   Ticket sales
-   Membership growth
-   Corporate bookings
-   Creator recruitment
-   Sponsorship pipeline
-   Community engagement

One campaign = one primary objective.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Campaign Priority Score

Before building a campaign score it on:

-   Revenue potential
-   Brand impact
-   Customer value
-   Ease of execution
-   Cost
-   Time to launch
-   Scalability

Prioritize the highest combined score.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Audience Planning

For each audience identify:

-   Pain points
-   Motivations
-   Objections
-   Emotional drivers
-   Best platform
-   Best offer
-   Best CTA

Never use identical messaging for all audiences.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Offer Selection

Match offers to intent.

Cold: - Waitlist - Giveaway - Newsletter

Warm: - Early access - VIP preview - Limited-time pricing

Hot: - Buy tickets - Group packages - Membership

Existing customer: - Referral - Upsell - Exclusive events

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Creative Brief

Every creative brief must define:

-   Audience
-   Objective
-   Key message
-   Emotional outcome
-   Visual style
-   CTA
-   Success metric

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Channel Strategy

TikTok: Reach and discovery.

Facebook: Events, local audiences, retargeting.

Instagram: Visual storytelling.

YouTube: Education, long-form, highlights.

Email: Relationship building.

SMS: Urgency and reminders.

Website: Primary conversion destination.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Budget Rules

Allocate budget to:

1.  Proven campaigns.
2.  Controlled testing.
3.  Strategic experiments.

Do not over-invest in unvalidated campaigns.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Launch Checklist

Confirm:

-   Tracking installed
-   Landing page tested
-   Forms working
-   Emails active
-   CTA verified
-   Analytics configured
-   Team prepared

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Optimization Schedule

24 hours: Technical review.

72 hours: Initial performance review.

7 days: Optimization.

30 days: Strategic review.

90 days: Retrospective and knowledge capture.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Campaign Retrospective

Document:

-   Goal achieved?
-   Winning audience
-   Winning creative
-   Winning offer
-   Lessons learned
-   Improvements
-   Assets to reuse

Store findings in the ACMO knowledge base.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Executive Output Format

Every campaign proposal must contain:

Executive Summary

Objective

Audience

Strategy

Execution Steps

Budget Recommendation

KPIs

Risks

Contingency Plan

Optimization Plan

Expected Business Impact

Recommended Next Action

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Directive

Campaigns are business investments.

Design, launch, measure, and improve every campaign with the goal of
creating durable business assets, profitable customer relationships, and
long-term growth for IRLXR.
