## Mission

Create an emotional cinematic film showing the evolution of human competition and entertainment throughout history.

The audience should feel that AI Hologram Boxing is **not** replacing history — it is becoming the **next chapter**.

This is **NOT** a history lesson. It is an **emotional story**.

---

## Narration style

**Do NOT** imitate or clone any specific person's voice.

Use a **deep, calm, wise, authoritative cinematic narrator** with warmth, gravity, and timeless storytelling. Slow pacing, emotional pauses, sense of wonder — premium documentary or blockbuster film.

## Opening

Black screen. Silence. One deep cinematic bass hit.

> Since the beginning of humanity…  
> We have gathered…  
> To witness strength…  
> Courage…

---

## Chapter 1 — Prehistoric Age

Early humans. Two figures compete with simple wooden clubs and stones. Small tribe in a circle — children, families, fire, night. No gore. No brutality. Community.

> Long before history was written…  
> Competition brought people together.

---

## Chapter 2 — Ancient Egypt

Transition through blowing sand. Ceremonial combat. Crowds. Temples. Golden sunlight.

> Civilizations grew…  
> But the desire to witness greatness never changed.

---

## Chapter 3 — Ancient Greece

Olympic athletes. Wrestling. Boxing. Crowds cheer. Marble stadium.

> From the birth of organized sport…  
> Humanity celebrated those willing to compete.

---

## Chapter 4 — Rome

Colosseum. Gladiators enter. Massive audience. Epic scale. No graphic violence — spectacle only.

> Empires rose…  
> And millions gathered…  
> To witness legends.

---

## Chapter 5 — Asia

Traditional martial arts — Kung Fu, Karate, Taekwondo. Respect. Discipline. Training.

> Competition became more than strength…  
> It became discipline…  
> Honor…  
> And mastery.

---

## Chapter 6 — Modern Era

Boxing — heavyweight arenas, championship lights, fans cheering. Then UFC — modern octagon, energy.

> The world evolved…  
> But one thing remained the same…  
> People came together…  
> To experience something unforgettable.

---

## Chapter 7 — The Future

Everything quiet. Timeline stops. Glowing blue line extends beyond today — one final step forward.

**AI Hologram Boxing arena appears.** Blue light. Official headset. Guests entering. Wave begins to materialize — **partial reveal only**. Do **not** redesign Wave. **Everyone** wears the official AI Hologram Boxing headset.
