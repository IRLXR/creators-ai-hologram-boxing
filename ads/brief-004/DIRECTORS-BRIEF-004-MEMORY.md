# DIRECTOR'S BRIEF 004 — CREATE A MEMORY

**Campaign:** Guest experience · emotional night-out commercial  
**Duration:** 60 seconds (target) · **16:9** hero + **9:16** cutdown  
**Status:** Pre-production  
**Models:** `nano_banana_2` → `seedance_2_0` (after frame approval)  
**Landing:** https://www.hologramboxing.com/landing.html  
**CTA:** Become a Founding Fan

---

## Mission

Do **not** create a commercial that feels like people are attending a boxing match.

Create a film that feels like they are experiencing **one of the most exciting nights of their lives**.

When someone finishes watching, they should feel:

> **"I want to experience that with my family and friends."**

If the film does not create that feeling — continue refining until it does.

**Director's final standard:** Do not create a commercial. **Create a memory.**

---

## Non-negotiables (brand + VISUAL-PROMPT-RULES)

| Rule | Application |
|------|-------------|
| **Venue** | Black **inflatable tent** with blue illuminated entrance — **not** a dome, octagon, or standalone arena |
| **Interior** | Dark quilted walls · glowing blue-purple **square on floor** · ground fog |
| **Headsets** | **Every visible guest** wears white Quest-style VR headset + luxury strap — zero bare heads |
| **Fighters** | **Wave** wireframe boxers, human 1:1 scale, inside the square — original matchups only |
| **Tone** | Authentic · documentary-real · nothing staged or ad-like |
| **Voice** | Deep, calm, timeless cinematic narrator — **do not** clone any specific person |

---

## Memory moments

Capture authentic emotional beats throughout the evening. Examples:

- Friends taking selfies outside the **glowing tent entrance** before entering
- A parent handing a child a bucket of popcorn
- Two friends laughing while walking toward the entrance
- Couples smiling as they enter together
- A family photo in front of the **illuminated inflatable tent** (blue entrance glow)
- Guests looking around in amazement as they enter for the first time — **headsets in hand or on**
- People naturally eating popcorn, hot dogs, and enjoying drinks while waiting
- Guests pointing excitedly as **Wave** begins to appear in the glowing square
- Friends celebrating together after the event
- Families talking excitedly while leaving

Everything should feel **authentic**. Nothing should feel **staged**.

## Emotional journey (audience)

| Phase | Guest feeling | Viewer feeling |
|-------|---------------|----------------|
| **Arrival** | "I've never seen anything like this." | Wonder · curiosity |
| **Entering** | "I want to know what's inside." | Anticipation |
| **Taking their seat** | "This looks incredible." | Awe |
| **Experience begins** | — | **"I wish I was there."** |
| **Event ends** | — | "That looked unforgettable." |
| **Final family shot** | — | **"I want to experience this with the people I care about."** |

---

## Final cinematic shot

The event has ended. Camera follows guests leaving.

- Some still hold empty popcorn buckets
- Some finish the last sip of drinks
- Friends keep talking excitedly
- Parents smile · children laugh · everyone genuinely happy

One family walks away together. A child (~8–10) holding a parent's hand **pauses**, turns back.

The **glowing inflatable tent** shines in the distance — blue entrance light on the black shell.

Blue light reflects across the child's face. Genuine wonder. Parent smiles back. They walk on.

Camera **cranes up** into the night sky. The **tent** becomes the visual centerpiece — urban night, city scale.

Music turns emotional and inspiring. **Slow fade to black.**
