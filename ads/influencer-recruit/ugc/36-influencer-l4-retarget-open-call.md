## Retargeting principles (why this differs from Script 1)

| L3 cold (Script 1) | L4 retarget (this script) |
|--------------------|---------------------------|
| Discovery — “WE WANT YOU!” | Recognition — “you checked us out” |
| Full offer pitch | One benefit reminder |
| “Sign up” | “Finish your application” |
| Broad open call energy | Warm comeback + light urgency |

**Do:** acknowledge prior visit · reduce friction · remind one concrete perk · keep tone inviting  
**Don't:** re-explain the whole concept · guilt-trip · use countdown panic (that's L5) · say “002” / “HB 002”
